The formula

CBM = L × W × H (in metres) × cartons
Volumetric weight = volume ÷ carrier divisor  →  kg (metric) or lb (imperial)
  • Dimensions convert to metres before multiplying. CBM is always m³, the global freight standard, whatever unit you type.
  • Enter dimensions in inches and the tool switches to pounds, since that is the US carrier convention. Enter cm or mm and it stays in kilograms.
  • Chargeable weight is the greater of actual and volumetric weight. Sea and LCL bill per CBM or per tonne, whichever is greater.
